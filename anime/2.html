#2. Attack on Titan

Summary

Several hundred years ago, humans were nearly exterminated by titans--creatures several stories tall, with no intelligence and a hunger for devouring human beings. To survive, humanity confined themselves in a city protected by extremely high walls.

In present time, humanity lives peacefully and protected, not having seen a titan in over a 100 years. Suddendly, everything changed. Eren Jaegar and his sister Mikasa Ackerman swore vengance upon the titans after witnessing something horrific as the city walls were destroyed by a colossal titan.

5 Stars
