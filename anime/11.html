#11. Fairy Tail

Summary

Natsu and his cat, Happy, traveled to the town of Hargeon in search of a dragon known as Igneel. They run into Lucy, a 17-year-old girl who wants to be a full-fledged mage. Lucy is determined to join the infamous mage guild, Fairy Tail. But, after being saved by Natsu, and learning that he is a memeber of Fairy Tail, and that there isn't much to loose, she joins Natsu and Happy on their futre adventures.
