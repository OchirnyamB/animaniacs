#18. XXX Holic

Summary

Kimihiro Watanuki is a young boy with a cursed gift. He has the ability to see evil spirits, and somehow they seem attracted to him. One day, after stumbling into a magic shop, Watanuki encounters Yuko Ichihara, a beautiful, seemingly all-knowing witch.

Yuko claims to have the means to end his torment, but she never works for free, and she wouldn't help him until he works for her enough to pay for her services. Thus he starts to work in her shop and discovers that in that place, nothing is what it seems.

Rating

4 Stars
