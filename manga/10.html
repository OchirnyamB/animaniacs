#10. Soul Eater

Summary

In the school, Death Weapon Meister Academy, students study to become meisters, those who use demon weapon companions with human and weapon forms. Maka Albarn, Black Star, and Death the Kid, seek to turn their weapons, Soul Eater, Tsubaki, and the Thompson sisters, into "death scythes" for Lord Death --the Grim Reaper and head of the DWMA-- by having their weapons consume the souls of 99 evil humans and one witch.

4.5 Stars
