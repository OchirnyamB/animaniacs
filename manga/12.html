#12. Kekkaishi

Summary

Yoshimori Sumimura is the next heir to the Sumimura family, as he lives as both a student by day and a kekkaishi --barrier master--. He must utilize barriers to defeat demons who wish to harness the power of the particular land they live around, Karasumori. As Yoshimori dwelves deeper into master the arts of kekkai to grow stronger and have the ability to safeguard those around him, the mysteries surrounding the origins of Karasumori slowly deepens.
